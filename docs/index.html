Addison Moore

Full Stack Developer | Product Designer

I'm a full stack developer and Bay Area Native. Before becoming a developer I worked as a museum curator and exhibition designer with a focus on interactive exhibition design. I worked to create engaging exhibitions that used technology to bring the museums where I worked into the 21st century.

I left the museum word in 2015 to become a product designer an experiential marketing company, where I worked on The Bosco's core products and designed digital experiences for brands such as Warner Brothers, Nickelodeon, Tommy Hillfiger, Facebook, Sephora and hundreds more.

After working with closely with the dev team at The Bosco and taking online web development course, I decided to hone my skills with the Web Develpement Immersive at General Assembly.
